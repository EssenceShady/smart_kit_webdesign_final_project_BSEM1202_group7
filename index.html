About Us

Smart Kit is a digital educational platform designed to support students in Sierra Leone by providing easy access to academic resources, examination materials, educational updates, and learning support services through a modern and responsive web platform. The platform was created to address the challenges many students face when trying to access reliable study materials, past examination papers, educational news, and learning resources. Smart Kit serves as a centralized hub where students can prepare for examinations, stay informed, and access educational resources anytime and anywhere.
